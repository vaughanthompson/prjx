Add Silence


You won't have the familiar engine noise to compete with when you operate our electric construction machines. So say goodbye to headaches and hello to your fellow workers while you enjoy a quiet day at work.

Add Clean


A world without harmful emissions is a better world for all of us. With our electric construction machines there are no more exhaust fumes to deal with, so you can work almost anywhere without a problem - even indoors.

Add Comfort


Vibrations are often connected to health issues like headaches, back pain and fatigue. Fortunately, vibrations are at a minimum in our electric construction machines, so you will feel better when you get home from work.

The perfect way to level-up
your green credentials



Electromobility by Volvo Construction Equipment has the power to transform the way you work. With the benefit of zero machine emissions, high performance and low noise, electric machines are the perfect partner to make any project more sustainable.


Switching one construction machine to electric is a good start towards fossil free sites. Our industry-leading electric machinery and chargers are available in various sizes and have been demonstrated to provide full-powered performance across a range of applications, spanning from urban construction and demolition to landscaping and agriculture.


At TDX we've been embarking on a company-wide sustainability programme for some time now. Together with our partner Volvo CE, a global leader in sustainable change - we are excited to expand our forward-thinking product lineup from hybrid to the next-gen, all-electric range of compact, high-quality construction equipment.

Charging of your Volvo Electric machine is easy - just plug in and power up. But knowing when to charge and how long for, in order to make the most of your operating time, depends on the type of work you are doing. This is why we have simplified the process with our easy-to-use runtime calculator. This tool will give you a quick indication on how your day could look like when using your normal breaks for charging. Put simply we are ensuring optimum uptime and productivity with reliable electric power systems

Efficiency now & long-term savings



Did you know that running your Volvo Electric machine is actually cheaper than operating a diesel alternative?

With the rising price of fuel, you can make cost savings on running a Volvo Electric machine, as it requires a simple power up from the electricity grid rather than using costly diesel. And as soon as the operator stops working, the electric motors turn off, so no unnecessary operating hours are added. So just imagine how much is possible to save over the time it takes to complete a whole project.

Indoor Construction

No Fumes. Thanks to their compact design, our small Volvo Electric machines are perfect for indoor demolition and construction. Nimble enough to get around tight corners, they also ensure a more pleasant environment in confined spaces, with zero exhaust emissions.

Outdoor Construction

After Hours.The lack of exhaust fumes emitted by Volvo Electric machines means you have the potential to work even within low emission zones. And thanks to their near silent operation, you also have the power to work through the night without disturbing people sleeping.

Utility

Sustainable Construction. Energy companies who want to make their projects more sustainable can look no further than our Volvo Electric Construction Equipment. Delivering the cost-effective power and performance they need, together with increased environmental care.

Landscaping

It is amazing what can be achieved when you work with nature and not against it. Zero-emission Volvo Electric Construction Equipment is a game changer for landscaping - not only for those working on site but for the protection of sensitive plant life.

Agriculture

Conservation and sustainability are a key focus for agriculture customers. Which is why many are turning to our silent zero-exhaust emission Volvo Electric machines to keep their business running while maintaining a peaceful environment for livestock.

Material handling

Volvo Electric machines are a perfect fit for material handling. Just as powerful as the diesel alternatives, they are capable of transporting different materials - whether pallets, earth or gravel - both indoors and outdoors, with a variety of attachments.

Waste & recycling

Our powerful Volvo Electric Construction Equipment are helping customers in waste and recycling fulfill their vision for a circular economy approach. They have the potential to lower costs and carbon, while being versatile enough to take on a variety of tasks without any impact on performance compared to their diesel equivalent machine.

Recreation

Zero-emission Volvo Electric Construction Equipment is good for the environment, good for business and good for society too. Imagine the difference silent electric construction makes at tourist attractions such as ski resorts, mountain bike parks and national parks.

Charging Compact Construction Equipment


From single phase overnight charging with an on-board charging cable through to three phase fast chargers that are portable and easy to use, we provide access to all the solutions you need.
  • Charge up to 80% in 1 to 1.5 hours
  • Up to a full 8 hour working day on a single charge
  • Charging options for both indoor and outdoor use
  • Chargers are portable with either AC (overnight) or DC (fast charging) options
View your charging options
Overnight charging cable 11kW AC Type 2

This charging cable is to be used for overnight charging after the workday. This options comes together with the machine, along with a variety of adapters covering all your needs.

9.6kW DC Fast Charger for indoor use

This is the fast DC charging station option with DIN plug.

17.3kW DC Fast Charger for indoor use

This 17.3kW DC Fast charger is a faster alternative to the 9.6kW option. It comes with DIN plug.

17.3kW DC Fast Charger for outdoor use

This 17.3kW DC outdoor charger with DIN plug, complies with Volvo CE's unique protocol and can withstand extreme outdoor temperatures. It is a "plug & play", just plug the cable into a 32A CEE socket.
