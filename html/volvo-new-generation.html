New technology that saves time and money

The latest Volvo excavator series helps you accomplish more while spending less. It's a straightforward equation that delivers significant results on the jobsite and your bottom line. With a range of advanced features, this new series boosts fuel efficiency by up to 15%, taking your productivity to the next level.

New electro-hydraulics: Improved electro-hydraulic system with an enhanced main control valve reduces your fuel consumption.

Outstanding cooling performance: Automatically adjusted fan speed minimizes unnecessary energy consumption.

Precision digging: Boundary limit controls allow you to work more efficiently and with greater precision.

Auto-boost: The hydraulic system automatically increases the working pressure to the boost pressure when pre-defined conditions are met.

Comfortable
Control

The cab offers a spacious, ergonomic, and quiet environment. Equipped with a variety of innovative features and smooth controls, it transforms the cabin into a truly modern workspace. A convenient storage compartment provides space for your cooler or shoes while you work. You'll appreciate the reduced noise levels, built-in sunshade, and upgraded HVAC system. Altogether, these features help operators feel less fatigued after a productive day on the job.

Stay charged and connected: In the cab, you can wirelessly charge your phone or use USB ports for charging and accessing music or podcasts. A Bluetooth speaker allows clear communication with colleagues or family while you work.

Easy-to-use technology: New features simplify your workday. The modern HMI offers quick access to settings with just a few clicks, while keyless engine start and an always-on camera enhance convenience and visibility.

Smooth control: The joystick provides smoother, more precise operation, enhancing machine performance. Customizable settings allow each operator to tailor controls to their preferences for optimal efficiency.

Fast and easy
servicing

Routine maintenance is key to extending your machine's lifespan and ensuring peak performance. But downtime costs money, so our latest excavator series includes design features that streamline servicing, saving you time and getting your machine back to work faster.

Extended service intervals: Volvo excavators feature industry-leading service intervals, with 1,000-hour changes for engine oil, hydraulic oil, and fuel filters. Synchronized service intervals at 1,000-hour increments boost uptime and lower costs.

Easy access: Grouped filters are easily reachable from the machine platform, and the EC230 features ground-level DEF/UREA filling. HVAC filters can also be replaced from inside the cab for added convenience.

Quick drain, fast fill: Engine and hydraulic oils can be easily drained via a quick drain port using a standard hose connection. Optional fast-filling systems are also available for these fluids, depending on the market.

Clean and quick oil changes: A shut-off valve during fuel filter changes prevents oil leaks, keeping technicians and the environment clean. An optional ground-level quick oil change port enhances safety and cleanliness.

Smart View with obstacle detection

Volvo Smart View with Obstacle Detection lets you focus on work while your Volvo looks out for you. It gives operators a 360° view of the machine's surroundings thanks to the camera and the new radar detection system.

New electro-hydraulics: Improved electro-hydraulic system with an enhanced main control valve reduces your fuel consumption.

Differentiates between human or object: On a busy site, operators may worry about injuring someone, damaging the machine or property at the site. Volvo Smart View with Obstacle Detection lets you focus on work while your Volvo keeps an eye on the surroundings.

Volvo Smart View with Obstacle Detection can identify obstacles as either an object or a human being, allowing the operator to take appropriate action. It gives operators an enhanced real-time view of their surroundings and automatic alerts on the high resolution monitor if obstacles are detected.
