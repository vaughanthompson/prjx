Volvo CE

We supply Volvo machinery to quarries & aggregates, mining, heavy infrastructure, utilities, road construction, building, demolition, forestry, industrial material handling & recycling industries.

The exceptional quality, performance and safety has lead Volvo construction to be one of the most trusted brands in the industry today. We take great pride in being the New Zealand authorised distributor for Volvo construction equipment, selling a variety of heavy machinery including; excavators, wheel loaders, articulated dump trucks and compactors. With years of knowledge and experience in providing equipment solutions, we understand the requirements of the industries that we work in. We are able to deliver expert advice to all our customers to help you select the best machine, whatever the application. Contact your local territory manager via your local TDX branch.

With nationwide branch support, whether your machine is in one of our workshops or out at your place, our service and parts teams will provide you with all the support you need and expect.

 

TDX

TDX was established 40 years ago and has grown from small beginnings to its current position today as a leading supplier of construction equipment, diesel engines, generators and transmissions to a variety of industries throughout New Zealand.

The company has been awarded the distributorships for some of the industries most highly respected brands in the world.

What we do

We are a leading supplier of construction equipment (including wheel loaders, haulers and excavators) NZ wide. New and used diesel engines, transmissions and generators for sale. OEM parts and oil and filtration products.

Vision & values

Our passion, truthfulness, teamwork and flexibility are values our customers appreciate. You can feel confident asking our team about anything from the smallest filters to the largest excavators for sale at any branch. With our shareholders working in the business we can provide our customers with quick decision making, years of industry experience, knowledge and expertise.

We provide an absolute solution for our clients' needs. From the purchase, through to the ownership and operating life of all of our products, we at TDX are renowned for our comprehensive service and parts support.

Nationwide

Having built an extensive network throughout both the North and South Islands our people are local, and understand our customers exact requirements, whatever their application, wherever they work.

Your specialists for construction equipment, whatever your application. Our range includes articulated haulers, wheel loaders, material handlers, compactors and diggers. NZ wide branches offer sales and service for all of our brands.

Est. ages ago.

Our story starts back in the late 70’s when three young, eager twenty something’s were over in the UK on their big OE.

Living and working in London as mechanics, they salvaged a transmission that was deemed un-repairable and was destined for the scrap heap. Over the next year, as and when they could afford to buy the needed parts, they set about rebuilding the transmission in their basement flat, followed by dismantling it, and rebuilding it in the back of a car and then driving it to a port to transport back to NZ. It was sold before it arrived back on our shores!

The rest is history and here we are today 40 odd years later, representing an incredible stable of brands and products and as a key player in multiple NZ markets.
