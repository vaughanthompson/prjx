Volvo New Generation Excavators, where Power Meets Precision.




Demo Session:
1-4pm Fri 8 May 2026
Join us in the Volvo Demo Zone at TDX Christchurch for an exclusive look at the Volvo New Generation Excavators, where power meets precision. During this hands-on session, the TDX team will showcase the latest technology and innovations designed to improve performance, efficiency, and operator comfort. You will also get a glimpse into the future with Volvo Electric excavators being on show too.
