BrainTree Wellness Centre Auctions



The Canterbury Brain Collective, a partnership between Multiple Sclerosis & Parkinson's Canterbury, and Dementia Canterbury - was established in 2017, with the goal of developing a wellness centre for local people living with neurological conditions.

This wellness centre is called BrainTree.
The new BrainTree facility provides a space for people and their whānau to connect with others who understand their conditions, as well as encourage engagement with the support services, information and activities available in their community.
