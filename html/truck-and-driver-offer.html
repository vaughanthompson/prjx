Rimula R4 L

Shell Rimula R4 L uses the latest generation technology to deliver significantly improved resistance to wear, deposits and oxidation. Based on high performance Group II base oils, it is one of the most advanced 15W-40 oil technologies available - and suitable for virtually all vehicles. That means that it's versatile enough to bring you the convenience of a single oil, suitable for virtually all engine ages and vehicle types.

Low emissions and reduced costs

Protecting the performance of your exhaust emissions control systems is not only important to ensure that your vehicles comply with legislative requirements, but also to ensure efficient operations. For example, blocked diesel particulate filters (DPF) can actually increase fuel consumption. Shell Rimula R4 L is formulated with reduced levels of ash and sulphur to help maintain the efficiency of the latest and future vehicle technologies.

Dobatex

Dobatex Gold is a water-based, multi-purpose detergent designed for industrial and automotive cleaning. From the heavy-duty demands of mining, oil and gas, and marine applications to truck fleets and routine janitorial cleaning, Dobatex Gold delivers exceptional performance.

Multi-Purpose Convenience: Effectively removes dirt, grease, and grime from all hard surfaces, including vertical surfaces, thanks to its stable foaming action that enhances penetration for a deeper clean.

Surface Safe & Rust Resistant: Won't promote rust or damage paintwork, polished surfaces, metal, or glass. Its strong colouring ensures easy identification even at low dilution levels, and it performs well even with poor water quality.

Powerful Cleaning Performance: Highly effective against a wide range of dirt, oils, and grease in both soft and hard water.

Streamlined Operations: A versatile cleaner that helps reduce the number of different detergents needed on-site.

Safe & Environmentally Friendly: Water-based, readily biodegradable, and non-flammable. Very low odor, making it ideal for workshops and underground maintenance stations. Free from hydrocarbon solvents and caustic alkalis. Classified as readily biodegradable under AS 4351 standards
