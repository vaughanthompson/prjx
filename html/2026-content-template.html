What we do

Comprehensive Strip & Assessment
Every component undergoes a dertailed strip-down and inspection process to identify wear, damage, and root causes of failure.

Torque Converter Rebuilding
Full-house capability to inspect, rebuild, and test torque converters for optimal performance and durability.

Failure Analysis & Reporting
We provide clear, detailed failure analysis reports- helping you understand not just what failed, but why.

Dyno Testing
Transmission Dyno Testing - Verifying shift quality, pressures, and performance under load.
Engine Dyno Testing - Ensuring power output, efficiency, and reliability before delivery.

Advanced Cleaning Processes
Using specialised chemical bath systems, we ensure all components are thouroughly cleaned and prepared for accurate inspection and rebuild.

Engineering Capability
Our engineering support allows us to tackle complex rebuild challenges, provide custom solutions and ensure components meet or exceed OEM specifications.

Precision Rebuild & Assembly
All components are rebuilt in a controlled clean room environment, ensuring contamination-free assembly to exacting standards.
