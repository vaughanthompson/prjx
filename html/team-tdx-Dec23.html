Hero Staff Member: Matt Williams, TDX Central and Lower North Island - Taupo


'Someone who not only helps our customers, but also makes TDX a better place'


Matt Williams has been with TDX for around five years and is based at the Taupo Branch. He started in the Parts Department and was soon promoted to the role of Customer Support Representative.

“Matt has really excelled in his CSR role, he's face to face with customers, works hard in the Aftersales area and represents TDX to an incredibly high standard,” said Joe Gradwell, General Manager - Aftersales Operations.

“He's not the type of person who just sits around and waits for something to happen, he's a real go-getter. He goes above and beyond, nothing is ever a problem and he's always willing to help others.”

Joe went on to say that customer satisfaction is the driving force behind Matt, he wants to see them happy and ensure they receive an outstanding level of service.

“He never wants to simply meet expectations, he aims to exceed them.”

Matt has recently taken on and stepped up to support key accounts, including Fulton Hogan. “He has taken ownership of situations and made them his own to resolve, following through on what needs to be done,” said Joe.


“The end result is a happy customer, one who enjoys a great relationship with TDX.”

Matt has also been one of the key people involved in the recent LDX launch, playing a pivotal part in making things happen from a product perspective. “He's got a great attitude, an outstanding work ethic and having him around helps make TDX a better place - he really lives our brand values,” said Joe.

“On a personal level, I'd like to thank Matt for his dedication to his job, he's someone I can rely on and really appreciate his support.
“It's people like him who make TDX the great brand that it is.”

High Five - Jordan Williams



1. How long have you been at TDX and where do you work?
I have been with TDX since late November and I work in the Christchurch Branch/Head office as the Finance Manager.

2. What is your favourite TDX machine and why?
I don't have a favourite yet but I'm very impressed with the R100E, it's a beast of a machine.

3. What is your favourite takeaway meal?
Love a good Indian curry, if you know any good spots then please reach out!

4. Tell us something people don't know about you...
I'm not your typical Accountant. When I'm not sitting behind a screen looking at numbers, I'm out in the workshop building Teardrop campers and working on old cars. Currently have a '65 Chrysler 300 and a '63 VW Kombi project..

5. What is your favourite TV programme?
Anything car related really. Currently enjoying watching Finnegan's Garage on YouTube. Wish I could weld as good as he does.
