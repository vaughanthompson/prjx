Locals helping Locals




At TDX we're as committed to our communties as we are to our customers. As a nationwide company, we're local where you are and incredibly proud to contribute resources and time to help out where we can. Check out some of these initiatives below.

But we're keen to do more! If you know of a community project that needs a hand...
Tell us about it

Proudly supporting ...


Southland Stags Rugby

At TDX we love our rugby, especially our Invercargill branch, which is why we are proud supporter and on the back of the Southland Stags number one jersey for the 2022 season. Go the mighty Stags!
Sydenham Rugby Club

Local rugby is where it all starts and in Christchurch we have been a long follower and supporter of the Sydenham Rugby Club. Well done to the boys on taking out the Metro Premier Final in 2022.
North Harbour Rugby

We love our rugby in the north also and North Harbour are doing us proud. We are on the back of the North Harbour number 12 jersey for the 2022 season. Good luck team!

South Island Wildlife Hospital

Sometimes it's the small things that make the biggest difference, like when we donated a whiteboard to help the South Island Wildlife Hospital in Christchurch. They do an amazing job treating and rehabilitating sick and injured native birds in the South Island.
Special Children's Christmas Party

Christmas is a magical time of year and TDX has been proud supporter of the Special Children's Christmas Party for many years. We want to see children happy and beaming with smiles for a day, regardless of the challenges they're experiencing. Merry Christmas everyone.
