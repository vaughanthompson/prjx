Health & Safety at TDX

At TDX we are committed to health and wellbeing. We care and take care to ensure all our people will return home in the same condition that they came to work. Our commitment to health and safety, it's backed up by our actions and the accreditations that we have received.



TDX has been awarded gold Sitewise accreditation which demonstrates the high quality health and safety systems we have in place throughout the business.



TDX is an ACC accredited employer at tertiary level which demonstrates the focus we have on our people's safety and wellbeing. ACC accreditation to Tertiary level can only be achieved when an employer has a clear history of established systems, procedures and processes that function actively in the workplace.



TDX is an IMPAC PREQUAL accredited company. Another demonstration of that our health and safety policies and procedures have been independently verified. An important measure for a number of field staff who are entering customer sites.


TDX. A New Zealand best managed company





TDX was judged to be a New Zealand Best Managed Company for 2023. The programme evaluates privately run companies in New Zealand against global benchmarks for success, backed by a 31-year history and a proven framework. TDX's inclusion in this recognised group underscores the company's commitment to excellence in management practices, innovation, and sustained growth.

According to the Best Managed Companies programme, successful companies such as TDX excel in four key categories which include strategy, culture and commitment, capabilities, and innovation, as well as governance and financials.

Sustainability

TDX is committed to working towards a more sustainable future.

As a leading supplier of construction equipment, diesel engines and transmissions, as well as consumable and lubricant products to the heavy-machinery industry, at TDX we are committed to reducing our carbon footprint and working towards a more sustainable future.

We have partnered with Toitū Envirocare.

With the guidance of Toitū Envirocare we have started our journey by analysing and understanding our carbon footprint. Our aim now is to focus on our reduction strategy through staff and supplier engagement.

Who is Toitū Envirocare?

Toitū Envirocare is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Manaaki Whenua - Landcare Research, a Government-owned Crown Research Institute. Enrolled in the Toitū carbonreduce certified programme, Toitū is providing TDX tailored software and technical guidance, independent audits and third-party certification. Everything they need to embark upon their carbon reduction journey.
